**Interlat** transmits all-position last-layer hidden states between agents via a learned adapter, achieving ~2,600× theoretical bandwidth over text and beating discrete CoT on the hardest MATH problems. Trained compression to 8 latent steps retains 94–96% of accuracy with a 46× speedup.
