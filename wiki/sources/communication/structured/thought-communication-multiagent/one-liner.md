**ThoughtComm** uses a sparsity-regularized autoencoder to extract disentangled latent factors from agent hidden states, decomposing them into shared (consensus) and private (agent-specific) components, with agreement-based routing. Provides identifiability guarantees that recovered factors correspond to true generative factors — adds *structure* to communication, perpendicular to the depth axis.
