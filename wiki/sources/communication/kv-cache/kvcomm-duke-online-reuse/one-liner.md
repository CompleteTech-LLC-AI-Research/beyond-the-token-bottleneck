**KVCOMM-online** is anchor-based KV-cache reuse for agents sharing overlapping context — estimating and correcting the offsets introduced by different system prompts. Achieves up to 7.8× prefill speedup (6.7× average) with <2.5% quality drop. A systems-level optimization orthogonal to the choice of communication method, composable with any KV-cache approach.
