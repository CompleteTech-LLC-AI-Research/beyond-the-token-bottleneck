**Cui et al. (2026)** test the BFS hypothesis directly on Coconut, CODI, SIM-CoT, and CoLaR. Confirms latent reasoning's *capacity* (Pass@100 advantage of 20+ points) but falsifies the iterative-BFS claim — distinct outcomes *decrease* with depth, attention bypasses latent tokens entirely on ProsQA, and stronger supervision fixes the shortcut at the cost of collapsing diversity (the **supervision–exploration trade-off**).
