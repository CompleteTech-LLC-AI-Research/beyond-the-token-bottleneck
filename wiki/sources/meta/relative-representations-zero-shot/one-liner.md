**Moschella et al.** show that well-trained networks produce latent spaces related by approximately **angle-preserving (isometric) transformations**, and that representing data as cosine similarities to shared anchors enables **zero-shot model stitching** with no learned mapping. Cross-architecture stitching jumps from 6% to 80%+ accuracy.
