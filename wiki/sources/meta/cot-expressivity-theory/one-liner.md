**Feng et al.** prove that bounded-depth transformers are limited to $\text{TC}^0$ circuits and *cannot* solve basic arithmetic without chain-of-thought, while CoT breaks this barrier by adding effective circuit depth. Establishes the **depth bottleneck** that motivates every mechanism in this field — discrete CoT, pause tokens, continuous thoughts, recurrence.
