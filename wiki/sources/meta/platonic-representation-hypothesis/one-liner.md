**Huh et al.** claim that all sufficiently capable models — across architectures, training objectives, and modalities — converge toward a shared **pointwise mutual information kernel** reflecting the statistical structure of reality, with higher-performing models aligning more tightly. Predicts that cross-model latent communication should get *easier* as models scale.
