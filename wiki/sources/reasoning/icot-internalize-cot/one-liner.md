**iCoT-SI** progressively removes explicit chain-of-thought tokens from a trained model, one token at a time from the left, forcing the model to compress reasoning into hidden states. The direct precursor to Coconut — Coconut's innovation was *replacing* removed tokens with continuous latent thoughts rather than just deleting them.
