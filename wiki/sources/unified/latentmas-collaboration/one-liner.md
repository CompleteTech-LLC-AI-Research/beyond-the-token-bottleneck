**LatentMAS** unifies latent reasoning and latent communication: each agent generates Coconut-style latent thoughts, then transfers its full layer-wise KV caches (including the latent thoughts) to the next agent, sharing the *complete working memory*. Training-free via ridge regression alignment; 471× theoretical compression over text and 4× faster than text-based MAS — but requires homogeneous architecture (catastrophically fails on LLaMA, −10.1%).
