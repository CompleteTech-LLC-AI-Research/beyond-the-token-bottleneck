**Vision Wormhole** repurposes the **visual input pathway** of VLMs as a universal continuous communication channel — a channel architecturally designed to accept dense continuous vectors — sidestepping the cross-architecture alignment problem entirely. Hub-and-spoke affine alignment (ridge regression on anchor texts) scales **O(N)** and works across fully heterogeneous VLM pools (Gemma, Qwen, SmolVLM, LFM): +6.3pp accuracy and 1.87× speedup over text-based MAS at the 1.6B–4B scale.
